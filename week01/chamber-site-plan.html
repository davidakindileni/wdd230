Site Name

Ile-Ife Chamber of Commerce

Ile-Ife (or Ifẹ̀ for short) is an ancient Yoruba city in south-western Nigeria. The city is located in present-day Osun State. Ife is about 218 kilometers northeast of Lagos with a population of over 500,000 people, which is the highest in Osun State according to population census of 2006. Wikipedia
The city is the ancestral home (or birth place) of the Yoruba race in South-west, Nigeria. The name represents the city itself wherein the chamber is located and operates.

Site Purpose

The purposes of this site are:

(1) To provide a platform for businessess within the city to come together and discuss matters of mutual interests to all members and their business.

(2) To organize workshops, trainings, seminars, trade fairs, exhibitions, business trips and any legal activity or venture that will contribute to the growth, expansion, and success of the business of its members.

(3) To act as one body in advising and influencing government policies and regulations in the best interest of members' businesses and the economy of the state and the country as a whole.

Scenarios

These are questions that a guest or a member of the organisation may ask when visiting the site:

(1) What are the requirements to join the chamber?

(2) What programs, activites or events are there for members of the chamber?

(3) What data or statistics does the chamber have on businesses and commerce activities in the city?

(4) What programs, activites or events are there for members of the chamber.

(5) Where and when are the chamber meetings held?

Normal paragraph example
